Refund & Return Policy

At Miracle Media Enterprise, we strive for complete customer satisfaction with our digital services.

Note: Since we provide digital services and consulting, "returns" are not physically possible. Instead, we offer a transparent refund process for unfulfilled services.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are strictly issued under the following condition:

  • If the paid service or digital product is not delivered or initiated within 2 business days of the confirmed payment.
  • If there is a confirmed technical error on our part that prevents you from accessing the service.

The Refund Process

1
Request: Email us at miraclemediaassistant@gmail.com.
2
Details: Include your Full Name and your Flutterwave Transaction ID in the email.
3
Review: Our team will review your request within 24 hours to confirm the non-delivery.

Timeline & Payout

Approved refunds will be processed via Flutterwave back to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, the funds typically reflect within 3 to 10 business days.

Miracle Media Enterprise reserves the right to refuse a refund if we find evidence of fraud or if the service was already successfully delivered.
